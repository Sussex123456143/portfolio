Foraging Alien: Instructions

  • Click the Green Flag
  • When the player character stops talking, the action starts!
  • Slide your mouse pointer over fruits to capture them.
  • By pressing S, you can sell the fruits to earn money! You need $500 total.
  • By pressing A, you can eat the fruits! Don't get too hungry, or you will lose money.
  • Watch out for DANGERCAT, your evil rival. Bop him with the mouse to knock him back.
  • Other events like the appears of motion bombs and breakable rocks will occur! Stay on your toes.
  • DANGERCAT's most dangerous trick is his last. Once you get over $500, you have to quickly stop and start the game by clicking the red and green buttons- otherwise, everything will reset and you cannot get to the ship.
